About Us

Barr Agency is a group of Real Estate professionals who, besides a deep knowledge of the real estate market, possess a passion for healthy lifestyles. Each specialist in our group has a hobby or talent in a healthy lifestyle activity. Their interests range from Yoga to travel, from healthy eating to Judo classes. Playing soccer or sporty fashion. Even fishing or playing music. Through this diversity we guarantee that our members are in tune with the individuals we represent.

When agents and clients share a passion for a specific life style we can be sure that the client finds the right property for them and that their new home meets all of their needs, even as those needs evolve during the home buying process.

Barr Agency invites you to begin your home buying journey now. Visit our facility and enjoy a drink at our fresh juice and coffee bar. Chat with us about your next property and let us help you find a home that fits your lifestyle. For sellers, we will be sure to highlight all of its unique highlights to prospective buyers.

Located in the north suburbs of Chicago, Barr Agency is bringing urban style to suburban living. We hope that you will let us bring a new level of sophistication to your next real estate transaction.
